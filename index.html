The Philosophy

Luxury is not necessarily loud. We create a unique balance between absolute opulence in design details and static calmness in the space. We are inspired by the ancient memory of the UAE desert and sea, translated into a contemporary design language.

Signature Elements

The Liquid Wall

Walls of cast gypsum or carved wood with organic lines inspired by desert sand, creating a dramatic play of light and shadow.

Light Sculpting

Contemporary Mashrabiya openings that allow light to paint moving patterns throughout the day.

The Material Surprise

Innovative use of traditional materials, such as "Marble Carpets" (marble flooring mimicking Bedouin rugs).
