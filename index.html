About AURA

At AURA, we see design as the art of building sanctuaries. Our philosophy is rooted in the concept of "Opulent Serenity": the delicate balance between bold luxury and absolute tranquility. We reject visual noise in favor of organic lines, natural materials, and exquisite details that whisper sophistication.

Our Vision

To become the primary reference for creating spaces that breathe, inspire, and immerse their inhabitants in a profound sense of peace and rootedness.

Our Core Values

  • Serenity: Commitment to designs that soothe the soul
  • Rooted: Preserving material authenticity and the warmth of place
  • Boldness: Challenging the conventional through masterful surprises in texture and light

We design life

The Philosophy

Luxury is not necessarily loud. We create a unique balance between absolute opulence in design details and static calmness in the space. We are inspired by the ancient memory of the UAE desert and sea, translated into a contemporary design language.

Signature Elements

The Liquid Wall

Walls of cast gypsum or carved wood with organic lines inspired by desert sand, creating a dramatic play of light and shadow.

Light Sculpting

Contemporary Mashrabiya openings that allow light to paint moving patterns throughout the day.

The Material Surprise

Innovative use of traditional materials, such as "Marble Carpets" (marble flooring mimicking Bedouin rugs).
